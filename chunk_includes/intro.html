Your task is to guess the rule and to decide whether the additional image at the bottom does or does not satisfy that rule.

Sometimes, the task will seem very easy. Sometimes, it will seem trickier if not entirely arbitrary. This is not an IQ test and there may actually not be a proper, unambiguous answer. What matters the most to us is that you follow your instinct and report your intuition, especially in these cases where it seems that you have to guess.
